The room was full of roses and quiet light.
A young man stood by the window, thoughtful and pale.
Beauty seemed to rest upon every object in the house.
A golden ray of sunlight touched the portrait on the wall.
Lord Henry smiled as if he knew a secret.
Art and youth were speaking silently to one another.
A strange feeling passed through the heart of the visitor.
The afternoon drifted by like perfume in warm air.
